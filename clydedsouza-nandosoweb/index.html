About Us

We have a long way in Aotearoa since 2000 and have expanded by heaps and bounds with the support of our franchisees. Our flame grilled chicken is no longer cooked in our backyards but in each and every Nandoso's restaurant. Each of our restaurants has its own unique style and comfort but what stays common is the bonding that we have with you- our customers! If you haven't experienced Nandoso yet, come join us now. It's never too late.
